About Noah

Noah Renken-Kapatos, age 24, is currently a senior at Illinois State University and receiving a bachelor’s of science in Audio Music Production through the emerging Creative Technologies Program.

Noah has a lengthy resume in independent music recording, composition, and production through releasing 8 albums and EPs since 2017 along with several singles in his independent musical artist career. These albums and singles range from acoustic/folk to hip hop, from indie rock to psychedelic, and from jazz to punk. In addition to writing, recording, and producing music, he has been a performing musician starting at age 15 in the Bloomington-Normal area in various bands. Noah has experience in traditional music composition as well as jazz guitar studies and performance at Heartland Community College’s Jazz Combo.

Currently is working at Eclipse Studio in Normal, IL as a producer and audio engineer with grammy nominated producer/audio engineer, Erik Nelson.

In addition, he is a music instructor at Hip Cat School of Music teaching music production, recording, song composition, and guitar.

Noah has prior experience in teaching private instruction on guitar, for traditional music theory and composition, as well as music production. He is a friendly face and is excited to help others manifest their musical passions!

Most importlantly Noah is the father of one of the best mini-lion in existence, Eddie Vedder Renken-Kapatos.

In his spare time he is a photographer and enjoys making comedic videos.
