Noah Renken-Kapatos

Noah is currently an undergraduate at Illinois State University studying Audio and Music Production in the Creative Technologies Program through the Wonsook Kim College of Fine Arts.

Currently works at Eclipse Studio in Normal, IL as a producer and audio engineer with grammy nominated producer/audio engineer, Erik Nelson.

In addition, he is a music instructor at Hip Cat School of Music teaching music production, recording, song composition, and guitar.

Noah also is also an accomplished musician and producer, having written, released, and recorded 8 albums and EPs since 2017 under the names Great Value Jesus and The Blank Sleeves.

In his spare time he is a photographer and enjoys making comedic videos.
